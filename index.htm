About

We are an Anglo–Italian family run business just a stone’s throw away from the majestic river Po. Our hostel is truly international with as many as 50 nationalities visiting annually.

Enea and Eva are 5th generation hoteliers, a brother and sister team whose mission is to put Calendasco fairly and sqarely on the Tourist Map of Emilia–Romagna.

60 beds in 15 rooms (mostly en-suite), chapel, lounge, patio, BBQ area, large mature gardens, free parking.

In four short seasons they have managed to generate a high volume of repeat business thanks to the warm welcome and personal service on offer at the Three Crowns Hostel, Bar and Restaurant, where customers can be sure of a truly excellent–value–for–money experience.

As we are situated slap–bang on the “Via Francigena”, the pilgrim trail that runs from Canterbury to Rome, we are a popular choice for walkers.

There are several musical instruments at your disposal, besides a large stage and dance floor for summer events. Our clients can also enjoy the local council open–air swimming pool complex, just 5 minutes’ walk away, from June to August, as well as tennis courts and riding schools nearby.

The haunted castle is on our street, as is the home of San Corrado, patron Saint of the village, now the delightful Knights Templar bar.

We have our own licenced bar and restaurant serving delicious freshly prepared local and international dishes and wines. From 2013 there will also be a self-service laundry room.
