Prices

We have a simple price structure at Tre Corone:

Bed prices (prices indicated are Per Person Per Night)

Dorm accommodation - € 17,00 pppn
Private room (5+ people) - € 17,00 pppn
Private room (4 people) - € 18,00 pppn
Private room (3 people) - € 19,00 pppn
Double/Twin room* - € 22,00 pppn
Single room - € 35,00 pppn

* En-suite rooms, double vs twin rooms and private bathrooms are subject to availability but the price is the same.
All prices are inclusive of tax.

Meal prices (not included in bed price)

Continental breakfast - € 4,50
German breakfast - € 5,50
English breakfast - € 8,50
Set menu (lunch or dinner) - around € 15,00
Half/Full board - check our menus, prebook and save!

Extras

Towel rental - € 2,00 per towel per stay
Laundry service - € 4,00 per load
Tumble drier service - € 3,50 per load

Discounts

Official Pilgrims - €5.00 discount off any accommodation
Children (up to the age of 4 inclusive) - free stay
Young guests (from the age of 5 to 10 inclusive) - 50%
Further discounts available for groups and for schools
Special negotiated prices for long stays Contact us for more details.

Cancellation policy

Bookings must be secured by credit card or 50% deposit
We will refund:
100% for cancellations made more than 2 weeks prior to the arrival date
50% for cancellations made between 2 days and 2 weeks prior to the arrival date
Cancellations made less than 2 days prior to arrival and no shows will not be refunded.

We accept all major credit cards. Short and long stays are welcome!
