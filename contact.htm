How to get here

GPS Coordinates

45.0877354, 9.5956831
N45°5.264124, E009°35.74098

Bus

We have a bus stop right outside the hostel; however there is no service on Sundays. Bus timetable is available on the TEMPI website (tempi.piacenza.it) The line is Piacenza–Boscone and tickets cost around €2.00 per trip.

Train

The closest train station is San Nicolò on the Piacenza–Turin line, about 1 mile as the crow flies. Piacenza train station is about 6 miles away and the bus stop is right outside the station.

Taxi

Taxis to/from Piacenza station cost around €25.00.

Car

Leave the motorway at Piacenza Ovest, turn right at the roundabout at the end of the slip road onto the dual–carriageway. Carry straight on for about 2 miles (through Sant’Antonio district) and go over the roundabout and over the River Trebbia bridge. Immediately after the bridge turn right on the bend. Turn left under the bridge and proceed for a mile or so turning left at the T–junction stop sign. Drive through Cotrebbia and Incrociata following signs for Calendasco. We are on the main street on your right, after you see the chemist, school, church, post office in that order. Drive straight into the double gateway at number 59.
